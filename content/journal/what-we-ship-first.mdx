---
title: What we ship first
description: The order of operations that keeps a site from collapsing.
date: 2026-02-23
tag: Process
---

A site feels real when the basics are true:

1. Routes work.
2. Content exists.
3. Navigation has no dead ends.
4. The build is stable.
5. Tests protect the experience.

Only then do we chase the last 10% of polish.
