## What makes it feel expensive?

A cinematic page has restraint. It earns attention with **pace**, not volume.

- Slow entrances
- Clear hierarchy
- Motion as punctuation
- Images treated like film stills

> The goal is not “more animation.” The goal is *confidence*.
